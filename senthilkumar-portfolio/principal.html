Key Achievements

Building Institutions

01
🏛️
Foundation of a College
Joined one year before commencement to establish regulatory foundations — navigating approvals across Madurai RJD, JD Chennai, Department of Higher Education, and State Secretariat. Secured MKU affiliation for 10+ programs across 4 phases including B.Sc. AI and BBA Aviation Management.
02
🚀
SPARK Mentorship Initiative
Launched the flagship "Spark" mentorship program (Slogan: Expert, Experience, Enrich) — 25+ programs featuring regional and national experts to drive student motivation and career development.
03
💼
Extraordinary Placement Success
Secured 80+ appointment orders for the inaugural batch of 19 students. Top students received up to 8 distinct job offers each. Trained students in Digital Marketing, FMCG Sales, Photography, and Media Anchoring.
04
🔬
GD Science Club & NASA Recognition
Established the GD Science Club — mentored student president Mr. Rajkumar to develop 120+ scientific innovations. A revolutionary ultra-high-speed data transfer technology (1,000 GB in fractions of a second) received recognition from NASA, ISRO, and the Saudi Space Agency.
05
🎯
NAAC Documentation & IQAC
Revolutionized institutional reporting — established IQAC, automated activity dashboards, and formulated SOPs for all 7 NAAC assessment criteria. Shifted from artificial event creation to authentic, rigorous documentation.
06
🎖️
NCC Army Wing Inauguration
Successfully established the 2(TN) Med NCC Army Wing on campus. First batch of 27 cadets produced 3 qualifiers for the national-level IDSSC Firing Competition — a significant achievement for a newly formed unit.
07
🌍
International MoUs
Established landmark partnerships with Shinawatra University (Thailand), Airport Authority of India, Kalaignar Centenary Library, IIT Madras (NPTEL), RK Group, Vinsup Pvt. Ltd., and 10+ other organizations across media, tech, aviation, and research sectors.
08
🏆
Student & Faculty Excellence
Students won ₹46,000 at the 2025 CM Sports Trophy. Student Mahalakshmi received Best Speech Award from the Virudhunagar District Collector. First prize at Mepco Schlenk Engineering College fine arts competition.
09
🏘️
Unnat Bharat Abhiyan — Rural Development
Directed adoption and development of 5 villages (Silamalaipatti, Sandaiyur, Peraiyur Bit I, Vannivelampatti) under the Central Government's UBA project. Improved rural infrastructure, digital literacy, and sustainable growth.

200+ Events Organised
Directed 200+ high-impact events including national/international seminars, cultural festivals, FDPs, Marathons, Yoga Competitions, Fashion Parades, Fancy Dress Competitions, and Women's Day Celebrations.

Formally established collaborative tie-up with IIT Madras — launched NPTEL framework on campus. Multiple students and faculty cleared elite IIT-certified national examinations in the inaugural cohort.

Leveraging cutting-edge AI tools to design future-ready curricula, optimize department performance, and drive campus digital visibility as a professional Digital Marketer.
