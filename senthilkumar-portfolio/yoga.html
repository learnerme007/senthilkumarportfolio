Beyond the
Classroom — Within.

Under the profound guidance of Guru Dr. K.P. Gunanithi (The Father of Energy Science), Dr. Senthilkumar has embraced the truth that happiness is the natural state of being and the ultimate catalyst for all human achievement.

In 2018, he achieved a Guinness World Record at the University of Madras by demonstrating yoga continuously for 59 hours without food or sleep — proving that the human body, guided by a disciplined mind, can transcend ordinary limits.

Today, he is committed to sharing this gift with society — offering training in Yoga Asanas, Pranayama, and Meditation to help others unlock their hidden potential.
