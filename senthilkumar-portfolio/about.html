Who I Am

Scholar. Leader.
Architect of Society.

Dr. P.S. Senthilkumar is a distinguished academician, institutional leader, and Guinness World Record holder with over 25 years of passionate commitment to education across Tamil Nadu.

Currently serving as Principal and Advisor at KR Arts and Science College, Peraiyur (Madurai), he brings together rare academic rigor, administrative excellence, and spiritual discipline rooted in the ancient wisdom of Yoga.

A proud alumnus of Madurai Kamaraj University, he earned his Ph.D. in Commerce and has since transformed numerous institutions — building regulatory frameworks, launching new degree programs, mentoring student entrepreneurs, and connecting colleges to IIT Madras through the NPTEL framework.

He also founded the Sharp Tuition Centre — running for over a decade — delivering personalized academic support to thousands of students in Madurai.

"Leadership is about creating the bridge between potential and achievement. As a Principal, I act as a facilitator dedicated to uncovering hidden talents."
