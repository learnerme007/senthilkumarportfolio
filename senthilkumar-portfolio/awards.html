🌍 Guinness World Record — Yoga

The Story Behind the Record

Under the profound guidance of Guru Dr. K.P. Gunanithi (The Father of Energy Science), Dr. Senthilkumar embraced the truth that human potential is limitless when guided by disciplined practice. This record was not merely a physical feat — it was a message to the world about the transformative power of Yoga. In 2018, he further extended this achievement with a 59-hour record, proving that the spirit only grows stronger with each challenge.
